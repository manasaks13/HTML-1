SIR M VISVESVARAYA INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

Sir M. Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology (Sir MVIT) is a well-regarded engineering college in Bangalore, Karnataka, established in 1986 by the Sri Krishnadevaraya Educational Trust, offering various undergraduate (B.E.) and postgraduate (M.Tech, MBA, MCA) programs, known for quality education, strong placements, and affiliation with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU). It's accredited by NAAC, recognized by AICTE, situated on a large campus near Yelahanka, and focuses on blending technical skills with management principles for holistic student development.
